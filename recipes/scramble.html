Scrambled Eggs Recipe

The end product!

This easy recipe will make sure your breakfast keeps the hunger away for a long time!

The best part is that you get to eat what you make!

    Ingredients
  • 4 eggs
  • Tablespoon of butter
  • Salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Finely chopped chives for garnish
  • 1 dl of milk (preferably whole milk)
    Steps:
  1. Break your four eggs into a bowl, and add the dl of milk into the bowl as well.
  2. Mix up the eggs and the milk.
  3. Add salt and pepper to taste into the bowl.
  4. Put your frying pan on low heat.
  5. Place the tablespoon of butter into the pan and let it melt.
  6. Pour your egg-milk mixture into the bowl.
  7. Begin dragging your spatula (I prefer using a flat wooden mixer for this as it doesn't scrape the pan) through the mixture from side to side. You can think of this process as if you were slicing up a pizza into six slices.
  8. The mixture will begin to coagulate into more solid chunks. The mixture will begin sticking to the pan. Simply keep scraping the material sticking to the pan into a nice pile in the middle of the pan. This is your scramble forming.
  9. Simply keep doing this process until your scramble reaches the consistency you yourself enjoy eating the most. Some like it creamier, others like it more on the solid side.
  10. Take your pan off the heat, and put your scrambled eggs on a plate.
  11. While the eggs cool off for a little bit, chop up some chives.
  12. Garnish your scramble with the chopped chives and enjoy a hearty breakfast!
